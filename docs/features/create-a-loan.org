#+title: Create A Loan

* Feature: Create a Loan
** Background:
*** Given a lender named "hidalgo" and a borrower named "aaron"
** Scenario: Lender creates a loan
*** When the lender creates a loan with the following details:
**** Borrower: "aaron"
**** Amount: $10,000
**** Annual interest rate: 12%
**** Term: 12 months
*** Then the lender is redirected to the Loan detail page
*** And the Loan detail page shows: borrower name, amount, rate, term, monthly payment, and start date
*** When the lender navigates to the Loans page
*** Then the loan appears in the list showing: borrower name, amount, and start date
** Scenario: Lender creates another loan
*** Given the lender already has existing loans
*** When the lender creates a new loan
*** Then the new loan appears on the Loans page alongside the existing loans
** Scenario: Loan requires borrower and amount
*** When the lender submits the loan form without selecting a borrower and without entering an amount
*** Then the form shows validation errors for borrower and amount
** Scenario: Interest-free loan is valid
*** When the lender creates a loan with 0% interest rate
*** Then the loan appears on the Loans page
** Scenario: Lender deletes a loan without payments
*** Given a loan with no recorded payments
*** When the lender deletes the loan
*** Then the loan is removed from the Loans page
** Scenario: Lender cannot delete a loan with payments
*** Given a loan with recorded payments
*** When the lender tries to delete the loan
*** Then the loan is not deleted and the lender sees an error message
